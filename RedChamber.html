Dream of the Red Chamber: Key Characters

Jia Baoyu (賈寶玉) The Protagonist

Born with a piece of jade in his mouth, he is a sensitive soul who prefers the company of women over the strict Confucian studies demanded by his father.

Lin Daiyu (林黛玉) The Tragic Love

Baoyu's sickly, brilliant, and emotionally fragile cousin. She is a gifted poet whose love for Baoyu is doomed by fate and family politics.

Xue Baochai (薛寶釵) The Ideal Maiden

Baoyu's other cousin. Tactful, worldly, and beautiful, she represents the Confucian ideal of a perfect wife and serves as a foil to Daiyu.

Wang Xifeng (王熙鳳) The Household Manager

A clever, capable, and sometimes ruthless woman in charge of the daily running of the massive Rongguo household.

Grandmother Jia (賈母) The Matriarch

The highest living authority in the family. She is a doting figure who protects Baoyu and Daiyu.

Jia Zheng (賈政) The Strict Father

A stern Confucian scholar and official who frequently disciplines Baoyu for his lack of interest in "noble" pursuits.

Shi Xiangyun (史湘雲) The Open-hearted Cousin

A cheerful, androgynous beauty and talented poet who often brings lightness to the household despite her own misfortunes.

Jia Yuanchun (賈元春) The Imperial Consort

Baoyu's elder sister whose prestige as the Emperor's favorite marks the peak of the family's power before its decline.

Jia Tanchun (賈探春) The Capable Sister

Baoyu’s outspoken and sharp-witted half-sister who briefly manages the family's finances with great skill.

Miaoyu (妙玉) The Aloof Nun

A beautiful, highly intelligent young nun living in the family's Buddhist cloister, known for her obsession with cleanliness and aloof nature.
